Vasuki

Vasuki is an Investor Club focussed on technologies disrupting the auto and broader industrials and mobility sectors.

It is a Club set up by the investors, for the investors.

About Us

Our Intent

Build a platform that brings together a network of corporate and individual LPs across the autotech and industrials value chain, sub sectors and segments gain access to early stage opportunities.

Our LPs

LPs represent the entire value chain from OEMs, Tier 1/ 2 suppliers to distributors.

Our Focus Area

We invest globally at all stages from Seed to Series B.
